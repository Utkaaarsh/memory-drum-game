how to play the game

  • press any key
  • remeber the drum that blinked and press that drum by moving the curser or using buttons
  • now again remeber the drum that blinked 2nd time and click the
    same pattern from starting with your mouse or keyboard buttons
  • For example :- lets say in level 0 drum w blinked,
    and in level 1 drum a blinked then you will press w and a similarly
    let's say in level 2 drum w blinked then you will w, a and w
  • Enjoy the game ...
